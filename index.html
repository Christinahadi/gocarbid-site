The Hidden Risk

A car can look profitable at the auction lane.

The real margin risk often appears after you've already won the vehicle. By the time a part is on backorder or recon takes twice as long as expected, the deal is working against you — and there's nothing you can do about it.

01
Parts are unavailable or backordered
Critical components — modules, assemblies, sensors — may have no ETA from any supplier. The car sits. The clock runs.
02
Repairs take longer than expected
Recon delays compound. The industry average time-to-frontline is 12–18 days. How close is your estimate?
03
Floor plan interest quietly erodes margin
At $40–$100 per vehicle per day, a 3-week parts delay can erase $500–$1,500 in expected gross before the car ever hits the lot.
04
Dealers need pre-bid visibility — not post-purchase regret
The decision window at the auction lane is tight. There's no time to research parts, check availability, or model carrying cost.
The GoCarBid Approach

Structured pre-bid intelligence before you commit.

GoCarBid doesn't replace your judgment. It gives you structured information to exercise it better — before you bid, not after you've won a vehicle you can't move.

01
Organize your auction watch units
Log vehicles across sourcing runs. Capture VIN, damage notes, MMR, and key acquisition details in one structured place.
02
Evaluate parts feasibility and repair exposure
Search for parts across eBay, Car-Part, and other sources. Flag scarcity signals, backorder risk, and repair cost exposure before bidding.
03
Account for turnaround time and carrying cost
Model floor plan interest, days-to-frontline risk, and holding cost exposure as part of your decision — not an afterthought.
04
Calculate a disciplined bid ceiling
GoCarBid produces a max-bid recommendation based on MMR, repair cost, target margin, and carrying cost — giving you a data-backed number to bid from.
05
Track results and improve over time
Archive won, lost, and passed units. Track actual spend versus expected margin across sourcing runs and months.
06
Decision support — not a black box
GoCarBid gives you the data. You make the call. The platform supports human-in-the-loop bidding, not automated decisions.
